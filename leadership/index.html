Coaching

At the age of 8, I never though one golf class later shape my perception of life and invaluable lessons. Golf teaches you there are many things out of your control – which directly affect you – and you need to embrace and accept that reality.

As a golf coach, I take this philosophy to each of my students. Building strong foundations, technical skills and knowledge for them to build upon our class and move further in their golfing journey.

Main Responsabilities

  • Provide 1:1 support and actionable tips for all participants
  • Host Event (Logistics and Planning)
  • Coach cohorts of 5-10 people simultaneously

Coaching Approach

Begginer Explanation Workflow: Basics (grip and stance) → Compare to sports (engage core) → Hitting position and biomechanics → Backswing (divided in two parts) → Physical cues → Claryfing questions (why certain positions, movement patterns and techniques yield different results in your final golf shot).
