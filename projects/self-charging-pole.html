Self-Charging Hiking Pole

Energy harvesting prototype combining magnetic induction and piezoelectric concepts into usable DC output.

Engineering Challenges

  • Mechanical-to-electrical energy conversion

  • Material selection (carbon fiber + resin)

What I Learned

  • Designing without a predefined solution
  • Balancing theoretical output vs real-world performance
  • User-centered thinking (emergency usability)
